INSTRUCTIONS FOR MASTERMIND ONLINE

Welcome to my online interpretation of the classic board game Mastermind! I built this out of frustration of not being able to play the game with my brother sitting thousands of miles away. As you've probably figured, my game allows you to play with your friends across the globe.

The starting screen asks you to input a room name and your own name, following which the first entrant into the room will choose between the CodeMaker (CM) and the CodeGuesser (CG):

On the next screen the CM must input his code for the round while the CG waits. After doing so, the CM will press the green arrow (which is used throughout the game as a submit button).

The game now starts! The CG makes his first guess (pressing the green button) and the CG chooses the red or white pins accordingly. The red thumbs-up button indicates a successful guess by the CG. The roles reverse, and round 2 begins!
